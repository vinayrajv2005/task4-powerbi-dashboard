# Task 4: Dashboard Design

## 📌 Objective

Design an interactive dashboard using Power BI to analyze sales data and provide meaningful business insights.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Power BI
* Dataset: Superstore Sales Dataset

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* **Total Sales:** 2.23M
* **Total Profit:** 283.14K
* **Total Orders:** 4898
* **Profit Margin:** 12.70%

---

## 📈 Visualizations Used

* Bar Chart – Sales by Category
* Pie Chart – Sales by Segment
* Line Chart – Sales Trend by Year
* Slicers – Region, Category, Order Date

---

## 🔍 Dashboard Features

* Interactive filters for better analysis
* Time-series analysis of sales growth
* Clear representation of business performance
* User-friendly and clean design

---

## 💡 Insights

* West region has the highest sales
* Technology category performs the best
* Sales show a consistent upward trend over the years
* Overall profit margin is around 12.7%
